About me

Im a user experience designer at Styl Fashion helping create an enterprise-level design application. I am a student at from UNC-Chapel Hill pursuing a B.S. in Information Science and Business Administration with a Human-Computer Interaction focus.

My passion for design started overseas, where I was exposed to different languages and cultures at a young age. I was fascinated by how design could transcend language barriers though I didnt think of it as eloquently back then! These experiences influenced me both professionally and personally as I focused my interests and areas of expertise at the intersection between humans and technology.

When Im not working, you can find me cheering on the UNC basketball team (Go Heels!), hiking, and film production.
